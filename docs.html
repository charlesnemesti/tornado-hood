Tornado Hood is a privacy mixer for Robinhood Chain. Deposit from wallet A, join an anonymity funnel of identical deposits, then withdraw to a fresh wallet B — with no on-chain link between the two.

Open app → Read the flow

How it works

The protocol breaks the deposit→withdraw trail using fixed-denomination pools and zk-style proofs. From the outside, every note in a pool looks the same.

01 · ENTER

Send ETH into a storm pool. You receive a secret note — the only key to your funds. Tornado Hood never custodies them.

02 · SPIN

Your deposit joins thousands of identical ones. Proofs show you own a deposit without revealing which one.

03 · EXIT

Redeem your note to any fresh Robinhood-chain address. A relayer can pay gas so the new wallet starts with zero history.

Tip: always withdraw to a brand-new wallet that has never interacted with your deposit address.

Wallet login

Use Connect Wallet in the header. Tornado Hood asks your browser wallet (MetaMask, Rabby, Coinbase Wallet, etc.) for an account, then optionally signs a short login message so only you can claim that session.

What we store

  • Your connected address (local session)
  • A signature payload for StormPoints / account UI
  • No password. No seed phrase. Ever.

Networks

Primary target: Robinhood Chain. If your wallet is on another network, the app will prompt you to switch when on-chain actions are available.

Fees & tax

The swap preview applies a 2% tax on the amount you enter.

  • YOU ENTER 1 ETH → Tax 0.02 → YOU EXIT 0.98 ETH
  • Displayed estimates update live as you type
  • Token contract address (CA) is TBA until launch
Preview numbers are for UI clarity. Final on-chain settlement will follow the live contract once the CA is published.

StormPoints

Connect before you mix to earn StormPoints. Roughly 1 point per ~$1 mixed. Points are loyalty credits for the community — not a cash balance and not withdrawable as ETH.

Safety notes

  • Double-check the withdraw address. Wrong address = unrecoverable funds.
  • Guard your secret note like a private key.
  • Prefer a fresh exit wallet with no prior history.
  • This product is experimental DeFi software. Use at your own risk.
  • Privacy tools may be restricted in your jurisdiction — know your local laws.

FAQ

Is Tornado Hood custodial?

No. Funds sit in the pool circuit; your note is the claim. We don’t ask for seed phrases.

Where did this launch?

Born on PONS, built for Robinhood Chain.
